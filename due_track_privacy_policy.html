DueTrack Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 13, 2026

DueTrack: Bills & Expiry Reminder is a Personal Due Manager by Naveen Labs. It helps you remember due dates, expiry dates, renewal dates, refill dates, service dates, payment dates, and other recurring responsibility dates.

1. Offline-first data

DueTrack is designed to work offline. Due Items, templates, settings, activity history, notification schedules, optional photo attachments, OCR text, and a hidden local profile ID are stored on your device.

DueTrack does not currently require login and does not currently sync your Due Items to a DueTrack cloud account.

2. Information you may save

You may choose to save information such as:

  • Due Item title, category, due date, due time, repeat rule, and reminder settings.
  • Optional amount, reference number, service provider, priority, and notes.
  • Optional photo attachments selected or scanned by you.
  • Optional OCR text extracted from selected or scanned images.
  • Activity history such as added, done, paid, renewed, refilled, skipped, snoozed, canceled, or deleted.

3. Photo attachments and OCR

DueTrack uses Android Photo Picker and ML Kit assisted entry to help reduce typing. Selected or scanned images are copied into app-private storage on your device. OCR text is shown to you for review, and extracted values are not silently saved into fields without your action.

DueTrack does not request broad storage access for attachments. The current app does not position itself as a document vault.

4. Notifications

DueTrack may request notification permission so it can show local due alerts. Notification permission is used only for reminders and summaries you enable. Some device battery settings may delay or restrict notifications.

5. Backups and imports

You may export a local backup file. Backup files can contain personal Due Item details and OCR text. Binary attachment image files and local attachment paths are not exported in the current version.

You control where exported backup files are saved and shared. Keep backup files somewhere you trust.

6. Ads and consent

DueTrack may show ads on non-critical screens. Google advertising SDKs may process device, app, and ad interaction data according to Google policies and the consent choices required in your region. Where required, DueTrack uses Google User Messaging Platform to show privacy and consent choices.

7. Data DueTrack does not access

DueTrack does not request or use:

  • SMS or call logs.
  • Contacts.
  • Background location.
  • Accessibility services.
  • Device admin access.
  • Broad/manage external storage permission.

8. Health and medicine reminders

DueTrack may remind you about medicine refill dates, appointment dates, lab follow-up dates, and similar due dates. DueTrack does not provide medical advice, diagnosis, treatment guidance, interaction checking, or life-critical alert guarantees.

9. Children

DueTrack is not directed to children. Do not use the app to intentionally store personal information about children unless you have the required rights and consent.

10. Changes to this policy

This policy may be updated as DueTrack adds features such as hosted sync, app lock, or additional ad controls. The updated date will be changed when the policy is updated.
