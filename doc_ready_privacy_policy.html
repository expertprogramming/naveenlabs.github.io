1. 100% Offline Processing & Privacy First

DocReady is designed with a strict privacy-first architecture. All core functionality—including PDF scanning, image compression, PDF merging, and document manipulation—is performed 100% locally on your device.

  • We do not upload your documents, images, or PDFs to any external servers.
  • We do not have access to the contents of the files you process using DocReady.

Third-Party Service Providers used by the app:

3. Permissions We Request

To provide our core features, DocReady requests the following device permissions:

  • Camera: Requested solely for the purpose of utilizing the document scanner. The images captured by the camera are processed locally on your device and are never transmitted to our servers.
  • Internet & Network State: Required exclusively to connect to Google AdMob to serve advertisements and Google Play Billing to process premium upgrades.

This Log Data may include information such as your device Internet Protocol ("IP") address, device name, operating system version, the configuration of the app when utilizing our Service, the time and date of your use of the Service, and other statistics. This data is used solely to identify and fix crashes.
