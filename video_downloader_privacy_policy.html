Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 18, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Video Downloader ("we", "our", or "the App"), developed by Naveen Labs, handles your information. By using the App, you agree to the terms of this policy.

1. No Personal Data Collection

We respect your privacy. Video Downloader does not collect, transmit, store, or sell any of your personally identifiable information (PII) on any external servers. There is no user registration or login required to use the App.

2. Permissions and Local Processing

The App requests and uses the following permissions solely to enable core downloading and file management capabilities on your device:

  • INTERNET: Required to parse links and download media streams from the URLs you paste.
  • READ/WRITE EXTERNAL STORAGE (Android 9 and below): Required to save downloaded video and audio files to your device's storage.
  • READ MEDIA VIDEO/IMAGES (Android 13+): Required to access, view, and save downloaded media directly into your device's public photo gallery.
  • AD_ID (Google Advertising ID): Used by advertising networks to serve relevant ads.

All processing, including downloading and merging video and audio streams via FFmpeg, is executed completely locally on your device.

3. Local Storage and History

The App records your download history (such as video titles, download dates, file sizes, and paths) to help you manage your downloads. This history is stored in a secure local database (Isar) on your device. We do not have access to this data. You can delete individual records or clear your entire download history at any time from the App Settings screen.

4. Advertising Networks

To keep the App free, we display ads using Google AdMob. AdMob may collect and use pseudonymous device identifiers (such as the Android Advertising ID or IP Address) to personalize ads, analyze ad performance, and prevent fraud. You can read Google AdMob's privacy policy here: Google Privacy & Terms.
