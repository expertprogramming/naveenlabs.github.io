Privacy Policy for DocReady

Effective date: 24 July 2026

Naveen Labs (“we”, “us” or “our”) provides the DocReady Android application. This policy explains how the released version handles information.

Local document processing

DocReady processes the documents, images, signatures and PDF files you select locally on your device. We do not operate a document-processing server, and DocReady does not upload your file contents to us.

Information we collect

We do not require an account and do not collect documents, signatures, extracted text, Recent Outputs metadata, advertising identifiers or purchase information in this release. Advertising and billing functionality is disabled and is not initialized.

Device permissions and file access

  • Camera: used only when you choose to capture a signature or scan document pages.
  • User-selected files and photos: Android system pickers provide access only to items you choose.

Generated images are saved to Pictures/DocReady and generated PDFs to Download/DocReady. Recent Outputs metadata and preferences remain on the device. App backup and device-to-device restoration are disabled. Removing the app clears app-private information, but exported files remain until you delete them using Android or another file manager.

Sharing and external links

Open and Share actions are initiated by you and send only the selected output to the app or service you choose. DocReady can open external privacy, licence, support and Google terms pages in your browser. Those destinations apply their own privacy policies.

Google on-device services

Where available, document scanning may use Google ML Kit delivered through Google Play services. Required scanner components may be downloaded by Google Play services. Scanning and document processing occur on the device; document contents are not uploaded to Naveen Labs. Google’s handling of service and diagnostic information is governed by the Google Privacy Policy.

Security

Your files remain subject to your device security, Android storage controls and the security of any destination you choose through Open or Share. No method of storage is completely risk-free, so protect access to your device and review sensitive outputs before sharing.

Children

DocReady is a general document utility and is not designed or marketed for children. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes

We may update this policy when the app or legal requirements change. The effective date above identifies the current version. Material changes will be published at this same public URL before the related app update is released.

Contact

Questions about privacy can be sent to support@naveenlabs.com.
