Privacy Policy

QR Mate — Developed and published by Naveen Labs

This Privacy Policy explains how the application uses permissions and handles information when you use the app.

Information We Collect

QR Mate does not require account registration and does not collect personally identifiable information for account creation.

Camera Permission

The Camera permission is used to scan QR codes and barcodes. Camera access is only used when users choose to use scanning functionality.

Contacts Permission

The application may request access to contacts for the following features:

  • Create contact QR codes from existing contacts.
  • Save scanned contact information into the device contact list.

Contact information is processed locally on the device and is not transmitted to Naveen Labs servers.

Storage and Local Data

The application may store generated QR codes, exported images, application settings, and scan history locally on the user's device.

Advertising

QR Mate uses Google AdMob to display advertisements.

Google and its partners may collect device identifiers, advertising identifiers, diagnostics information, and usage information according to their own privacy policies.

Google Privacy Policy:
https://policies.google.com/privacy

Data Sharing

Naveen Labs does not sell personal information.

The application does not transmit contact information to Naveen Labs servers.

Third-party services such as Google AdMob may process information according to their own privacy policies.

Data Security

The application is designed to process QR codes, barcode information, and contact information locally on the device whenever possible.

Children's Privacy

The application is not directed toward children under the age of 13.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically. Any changes will be published on this page.
